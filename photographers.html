How My Photographer Ruined My Wedding Day

Wedding Planning 19 Jan, 2018

Image Source: Google Photos

It was already 9.30 am, my makeup artist friends and others were in the house with me but guess who I have spent the last 30 min call? Jide the Photographer.

I found Jide on instagram, I saw his works and I loved it. I compared it with some other works on instagram but I felt he was the man for the job. We spoke over Dm and he sent me some more works. I felt hey! This is good, we negotiated and I sent my driver to check up his office space. He came back with confirmation, so I made 80% payment.

Jide rushed in with his assistant telling me he had some issue with traffic and I told him I won’t take such excuse, he begged for a while and my friends pacified me saying it’s my day, we should just let it go. I did and we continued the day with him taking shots. I was enjoying the moment, yes I am vain like that. I also heard his boy arrived at my fiance's place pretty late. He, my fiance didn’t really bother querying him and that infuriated me.

You see everything was now in motion and going as planned for me to get my dream wedding, I was excited, my friends were excited, it was a beautiful day, the sun and the moon were aligned just for me to shine.

We were ready to leave for the church, everyone was packed in the cars everything was moving fine until we realised the Photographer didn't join us in the car. The dude according to him mistakenly missed the convoy, I picked up my phone and called repeatedly; “The number you are calling is switched off”. My friends tried to calm me down, I tried my best to stay calm and composed but my gut won’t let me.

I had to walk down the aisle without my photographer, my eyes could not control my tears, it’s my big day, my big walk in and no professional photographer to document it. We only had his assist who was with my fiancee. I had a hunch the assist was a crappy photographer I just wanted to believe I was paranoid.

Image Source: Google Photos

It turns out, Jide’s phone went off before we left the house and he didn’t know the location of the church, so he was looking for where he would charge the phone, wait for it to charge before he could make it to the church. He arrived just after I had walked up the aisle and started running around snapping. It was my husband who helped me contain my anger and not blowing up there, I had tears rolling out of my eyes and he kept telling me that it’s our moment and I shouldn’t let the world ruin it.

I would have loved to say that was the end of my trauma, I swear I wished that was it, I wish it ended with that day but no, 2 months later after disturbing him repeatedly he reluctantly sends the pictures he has worked. All hell broke loose in my head. I could feel my tears turn to magma, my soul ripped apart from my body. I couldn’t select one picture that made sense. Not one, everything was complete rubbish.

There are things you could be refunded in life, photographs is not one of them. My ordeal with Jide ruined my wedding day, it ruined MY LIFE!!
