It is You and I, Always! Ribbons Bride Gracey and Prince Charming Dasoye’s Wedding #GraDas18

realwedding 11 Aug, 2018

Gracey and Dasoye tied the knot in a beautiful ceremony filled with lots of fun and joy as the couple embraced spending their life together in the presence of their family, friends, and well-wishers.

Our Ribbons bride rocked the day in a beautiful white wedding dresswhile her groom looked dapper in his royal blue suit. Their traditional wedding ceremony was everything beautiful, as the couple dazzled in their traditional attires. The bride later changed into a blue Aso-oke outfit and the groom into a blue outfit.
